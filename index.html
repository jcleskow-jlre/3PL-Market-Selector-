Methodology

This rebuild reframes the tool as a 3PL network decision platform rather than a generic real estate ranking. The model separates five business questions: overall market attractiveness, transportation infrastructure, labor availability, real estate economics, renewal leverage and network strategy.

Transportation infrastructure

Scores port access/throughput, intermodal connectivity, parcel hubs, interstate density and air cargo relevance. This is critical because transportation often outweighs rent in total logistics cost.

Renewal leverage

Scores where tenants are most likely to have negotiating power: higher vacancy, more construction, more sublease/shadow space, weaker absorption and landlord/developer exposure.

Labor

Designed to incorporate BLS OEWS occupation wages and employment, QCEW warehousing/trucking employment, wage pressure and labor availability.

Real estate economics

Uses Savills market fundamentals: asking rent, vacancy, inventory, under construction, deliveries and net absorption.
